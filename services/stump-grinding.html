Stump Grinding

Remove unsightly stumps quickly and efficiently.

Schedule this service

Overview

Leftover stumps can be tripping hazards, attract pests, and ruin your lawn's appearance. Our stump grinding service uses powerful equipment to grind stumps below ground level, allowing you to replant or sod over the area. We clean up all wood chips and level the site.

What's included

  • Eliminates tripping hazards in your yard
  • Prevents termites and other pests from nesting
  • Frees up space for new landscaping

How it works

1

Site Prep

We clear debris and ensure safe access for the grinder.

2

Grinding

The stump is ground down to 6-12 inches below grade using a professional stump grinder.

3

Finish

We backfill the hole with soil and remove all wood chips.

Common questions

How deep do you grind stumps?

We typically grind 6-12 inches below ground level, which is sufficient for most landscaping needs.

Can I plant a tree in the same spot after grinding?

Yes, but we recommend waiting a few months or replacing the soil to prevent nutrient issues.
